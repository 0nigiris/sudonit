The goal is simple:

A visitor should understand who Alexander is within the first 15 seconds.

The website should feel like a modern product presentation.

The website should tell a story.

Not explain a navigation system.

## Project Showcase

This is the single "wow" section.

Projects rotate around a circular path.

Important:

The circle is viewed from an angled front perspective.

Not from above.

The visitor sees depth.

Front cards appear larger.

Back cards appear smaller.

The rotation is controlled by scrolling.

After one complete rotation, the page continues normally.

Projects:

* Computer Repair
* Tutoring
* English Project
* Smart Glasses Project

Selecting a project opens a dedicated page.
