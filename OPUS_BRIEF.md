## Critical Requirements

The previous version failed because it focused on the interface instead of the person.

The new version must focus on Alexander.

Visitors should learn:

* who he is
* what he does
* what he builds
* how to contact him

before making any navigation decisions.

---

## Scroll First

Scrolling is the primary interaction.

The homepage should feel natural.

The visitor should move through a story.

Avoid:

* command palettes
* hidden interactions
* keyboard shortcuts
* dashboard metaphors
* operating-system metaphors
